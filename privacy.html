Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

NewSites.com ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered business automation platform and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and business information you provide when signing up or using our services.
  • Business Data: Lead information, contact details, call recordings, SMS messages, and CRM data associated with your business account.
  • Social Media Data: When you connect your social media accounts, we collect access tokens and page information necessary to post content on your behalf.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, including log data, IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited.
  • Communications: Records of calls, SMS messages, and other communications processed through our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our AI-powered business automation services
  • Process and manage leads on your behalf
  • Send automated SMS messages and manage call routing for your business
  • Post content to your connected social media accounts as directed by you
  • Send you notifications about hot leads and important business events
  • Improve and personalize your experience on our platform
  • Communicate with you about your account and our services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Social Media Integrations

When you connect your Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X, or Google Business accounts to NewSites.com:

  • We access your social media pages and accounts solely to post content on your behalf as directed by you or your automated posting schedule.
  • We store access tokens securely on our servers to enable automated posting.
  • We do not sell your social media data to third parties.
  • You can disconnect your social media accounts at any time through your account settings.
  • Our use of data from Meta platforms complies with Meta's Platform Terms and Developer Policies.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist us in providing our services (e.g., Twilio for SMS/calls, Google for AI processing).
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of certain data processing activities
  • Data portability

8. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience on our platform. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

9. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with third-party services including Twilio, Google, Meta (Facebook/Instagram), and Twitter/X. Your use of these integrations is also subject to their respective privacy policies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
